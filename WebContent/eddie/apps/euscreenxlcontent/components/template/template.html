EUscreen Content Selection

The EUscreen portal provides access to television clips and programmes from broadcasters and broadcast archives from 24 European countries. The EUscreen collection contains nearly 60 000 media items: mostly videos, but it also included photographs, audio files and documents. All items have been hand-picked by experienced archivists and researchers for inclusion in this collection. Click on a country to find out more about the EUscreen collection!
